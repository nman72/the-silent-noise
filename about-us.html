The Silent Noise is a small Indie Alternative band from the mid-west. Coming from a family of classical musicians, Steve and Andrew Watson started The Silent Noise as a guitar and drum duo inspired from their parent's previous work. After their first album they expanded their band to a 4 piece band, which included Fredrick Shaw and Ryan Hopper, who had been following the bands work throughout the development and tour of the first album.


Five albums into their career they still sell out small venue shows across the country as well as international shows in Europe. Due to the bands usual small venue shows it's very easy for them to engage with fans and talk to everyone, instead of the common trend of paying extra for VIP tickets to shake hands and take a picture with a "larger than life" band or musician. This type of engagement really resonates with the band as they really appreciate their fans and like having conversations and sharing personal life events, like a friend or family member.
